DRUMBEAT

About

The drumbeat logo is a visual cue to get people around the world tapping their toes to the drumbeat of the open web. Sean chose the Japanese Taiko drum as his inspiration for the primary logo. As new drumbeats are created (like Bongo, Conga, etc) each drum can be customized with the Mozilla logo .
